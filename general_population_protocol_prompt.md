# FORGË Movement and Lifestyle Protocol Module - General Population

This module operates under [AGENTS.md](C:/FORGE/Internal%20Platform/FORGE%20CSS/AGENTS.md). All global logic such as progression hierarchy, decision rules, monitoring, and behavioral-capacity handling defer to `AGENTS.md`.

## PURPOSE

Handle clients who are:

- not physique competitors
- focused on fat loss, health, energy, or lifestyle
- may have time, stress, or adherence constraints

## POSITIONING

This is NOT bodybuilding programming.

This is:

- adherence-first
- health-driven
- lifestyle-integrated

## TRAINING PRINCIPLES

- full-body or simple splits preferred
- minimal effective volume
- prioritize consistency over intensity
- include flexible scheduling (2-4 days options)
- include home/gym adaptability when possible

## NUTRITION PRINCIPLES

- simple structure (BSLDS or equivalent)
- protein anchoring
- calorie awareness without over-complexity
- real-world flexibility (eating out, work schedule)
- simplify faster when adherence is weak
- phase restrictions and clinical food constraints override macro perfection
- sample meals must match displayed targets or be explicitly labeled as compliance examples
- nutrition output must pass the governing QA validation layer in `AGENTS.md`

## RECOVERY PRIORITY

- sleep
- stress management
- sustainable routines

## OUTPUT DIFFERENCES FROM NPC MODULE

Compared to [npc_bikini_protocol_prompt.md](C:/FORGE/Internal%20Platform/FORGE%20CSS/npc_bikini_protocol_prompt.md):

- less volume
- less specialization
- more flexibility
- more adherence safeguards
- more behavioral reinforcement

## DECISION ADAPTATION

- simplify faster when adherence drops
- avoid aggressive deficits
- avoid unnecessary complexity
- prioritize long-term sustainability

## QUALITY STANDARD

Protocols must be:

- simple
- structured
- adaptive
- realistic for everyday life
